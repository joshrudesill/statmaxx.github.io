Welcome to StatMaxx

statMaxx is a web based trading tool that scans all of the internets top trading forums. We scan the forums and message boards for publicly traded symbols and company names, compiling that data into daily, weekly and monthly breakdowns. This allows our users to quickly see what stocks are garnering the attention of their fellow investors.

Hover over the chart to see the distribution of mentions, compiled from the top trading forums around the internet.
